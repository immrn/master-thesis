Setup Two Factor Authentication

  1. Login at example.com .
  2. Have a look at the settings of the webservice for options like Two Factor Authentication or Security.
    Some webservices will force you to setup the second factor, while you are logging in for the first time.
  3. When the website displays a QR code, continue here.
You didn't find a QR code?
Then the webservice might not support the two factor authentication we need. So you can't use Blue TOTP on this webservice.
